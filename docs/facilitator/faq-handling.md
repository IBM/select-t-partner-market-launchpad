# FAQ Handling

Common questions you'll hear during a session, and suggested responses.

---

## During Theory

**"How does this compare to [competitor product]?"**
> Acknowledge the question, give a direct differentiator, and offer to go deeper after the session. Don't get pulled into a lengthy comparison during theory.

**"Do we need to be certified to sell this?"**
> Clarify the partner programme requirements. If unsure, note it and follow up after the session.

**"Can we see a real customer example?"**
> Use one of the Use Cases from the theory module. If you have a sanitised case study, this is a great moment to share it.

---

## During Labs

**"I'm getting an error not in the troubleshooting doc."**
> Ask to see the full error. Check the [Troubleshooting](../tracks/track-01/troubleshooting.md) page first, then assist directly. Log new issues to be added to the doc after the session.

**"Can I skip ahead to Lab 401?"**
> Encourage following the sequence — each lab builds on the last. If an attendee is significantly faster than the group, give them an extension challenge rather than letting them skip.

**"Will this work in my client's existing environment?"**
> Note this as a great post-session follow-up. Collect the specific context (cloud provider, infra constraints) and connect them with the right technical resource.

---

## After the Session

**"Where can I find this content again?"**
> Point them to this MkDocs site URL.

**"When is the next session?"**
> Collect their interest and feed it back to the program organiser.

**"Can we get a recording?"**
> Partner Market Launchpad is intentionally in-person and not recorded — the value is in the hands-on experience, not passive consumption. Offer the docs site as the reference instead.
