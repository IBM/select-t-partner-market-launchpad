# Lab 101 — Legal Domain

!!! info "Domain Context"
    In this lab, you'll build a foundational legal document management solution. You'll learn how to organize legal documents, manage contracts, and create basic compliance tracking using the product's core features.

## Scenario

You're working with **LegalTech Partners**, a law firm looking to modernize their document management and contract lifecycle. They need a solution to organize case files, track contract statuses, and ensure compliance with legal requirements.

## What You'll Build

- Legal document repository and classification system
- Contract lifecycle management workflow
- Basic compliance tracking functionality
- Document search and retrieval interface

## Business Outcomes

By completing this lab, you'll help LegalTech Partners:

- Centralize legal documents across all practice areas
- Reduce contract review time by 40%
- Improve compliance tracking and reporting
- Streamline document retrieval and access

## Technical Components

You'll work with:

- Document data models and metadata schemas
- Contract workflow APIs
- Document search and classification
- Basic integration patterns
