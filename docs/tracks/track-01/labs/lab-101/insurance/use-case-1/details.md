# AI-Powered Insurance Broker Assistant

## Overview

In the insurance industry, broker productivity depends on fast access to accurate customer and policy data. Brokers spend significant time switching between systems, manually querying databases, and compiling policy summaries — time that could be spent on higher-value client interactions.

The AI-Powered Insurance Broker Assistant enables brokers to retrieve customer profiles, policy details, premium summaries, and renewal alerts through a conversational AI interface embedded directly in the brokerage portal. Brokers can ask questions in plain English and receive structured, actionable responses — without navigating multiple backend systems.

The solution integrates with existing policy management APIs, ensuring real-time data accuracy and consistent information delivery across the Orchestrate Chat interface and the brokerage web portal.

## Benefits

- **Broker Productivity:** Eliminates time spent navigating multiple systems to find customer and policy information. Brokers get instant answers to routine queries — such as policy status, premium totals, and renewal deadlines — through a single conversational interface.
- **Faster Client Service:** Reduces the time-to-answer for common broker queries from minutes to seconds. Brokers can respond to client calls with accurate, up-to-date information without placing clients on hold.
- **Reduced Errors:** Delegates calculations such as total premium aggregation to purpose-built API tools rather than manual arithmetic, eliminating a common source of human error in financial reporting.
- **Scalability:** The agent can be deployed across teams and embedded into any web-based portal with a single script snippet. New data sources and API operations can be added as tools without rebuilding the agent from scratch.
- **Data-Driven Insights:** Agent Analytics captures every interaction as a traceable event, providing visibility into query volume, response latency, and failure rates — enabling continuous improvement of the agent and the underlying data services.
- **Consistency & Compliance:** Ensures brokers receive policy-compliant, standardised responses regardless of which team member is making the query, reducing the risk of inconsistent client communications.
