# Mode of Conduct

Partner Market Launchpad is delivered **in-person** for maximum impact. Face-to-face collaboration, real-time lab support, and peer learning drive partner confidence and retention in ways that virtual delivery cannot replicate.

---

## Delivery Options

=== "Option A: 2 Half-Day Sessions"

    **Best for:** Teams who benefit from overnight reflection between theory and labs, or where full-day availability is difficult to secure.

    | Session | Time | Content |
    |---------|------|---------|
    | **Session 1** | Morning (~4 hrs) | Theory deep-dive + Lab 101 + Lab 201 |
    | **Session 2** | Morning (~4 hrs) | Lab 301 + Lab 401 + Lab 501 (Capstone) |

    !!! tip "Retention Advantage"
        Splitting across two days gives attendees time to reflect, explore independently, and arrive at Session 2 with sharper questions.

=== "Option B: 1 Full-Day Session"

    **Best for:** Teams who want complete enablement in a single, intensive day.

    | Block | Time | Content |
    |-------|------|---------|
    | **Morning** | 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM | Theory + Lab 101 + Lab 201 + Q&A |
    | **Lunch Break** | 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM | Networking & informal discussion |
    | **Afternoon** | 2:00 PM – 6:00 PM | Lab 301 + Lab 401 + Lab 501 (Capstone) |

    !!! tip "Intensity Advantage"
        A single day keeps momentum high and eliminates scheduling friction across multiple calendar blocks.

---

## Why In-Person?

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Real-time lab support** | Facilitators can unblock attendees immediately — no waiting for async responses |
| **Peer learning** | Attendees solve problems together, sharing approaches and shortcuts |
| **Partner–client rapport** | Partners and client-side leaders build trust in a shared experience |
| **Focus & immersion** | No Slack, no email, no context switching — full attention on the product |

---

## Venue Recommendations

- A room that supports **breakout-style seating** (not theatre/lecture)
- **One laptop per attendee** with internet access for lab environments
- A **large display or projector** for facilitator demos
- Whiteboards or flip charts for theory sessions and Q&A capture
